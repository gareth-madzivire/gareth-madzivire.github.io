Background & context

Zimbabwe forces clarity. Inflation, multi-currency accounting, fragmented tools, regulatory ambiguity, and connectivity gaps punish weak workflows. My work is a direct response to these realities.

Why I build

Early in my career I learned the limiting factor wasn't accounting knowledge. It was the absence of dependable, context-aware systems. So I started building tools that respect how work is actually done here: offline, under pressure, with imperfect data — and with high compliance consequences.

Core values
